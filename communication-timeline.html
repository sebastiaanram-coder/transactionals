Reading this timeline. Each track runs on its own clock: the order journey is relative to checkout and to order events (proof, shipment); billing is relative to the invoice due date; account messages are event-driven. Timings shown are when each message fires. Conditional messages only send when their condition is met (e.g. reminders only if an action is still open). Email is the floor on every billing step; SMS rides on top for the urgent dunning touches (second reminder, collection 1 & 2), and a posted letter adds legal weight on the notice of default and collections 1 & 3. The finance plan also defines a value-gated phone call (+46, claims ≥ €75) and a write-off / judicial-collection decision (+53) — internal steps, not customer messages, so they are not shown here.
